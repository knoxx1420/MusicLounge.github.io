ARTIST SPOTLIGHT

Join us and discover the most influential bands and artists of the history of music every month. This time we have:

TOOL is often characterized as being one of the most obscure, sophisticated and celebrated acts in rock. Having a progressive metal foundation, the Los Angeles group hasn’t done themselves any favors in terms of marketing towards the wider public, but they’ve found success in the excellence of their unique songwriting and provoking imagery. The four piece consisting of Maynard James Keenan, Adam Jones, Danny Carey and Justin Chancellor formed in 1990 and have since gone on to be an arena selling, multi platinum and Grammy award winning act.

While the band is currently active and touring, their latest record 2019’s Fear Inoculum happened to be the groups first studio album in over 13 years. Hopefully fans won’t have to wait longer for the band’s next musical endeavor, but if they do it’ll likely follow in the steps of Fear Inoculum and be a number 1 charting album.
